Privacy Policy for Sat Math

Effective Date: 18 November 2024

Sat Math (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Sat Math mobile application (“App”) handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

The Sat Math app does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The App:

  • Does not connect to external servers for user data
  • Does not collect analytics data from users
  • Does not use tracking technologies
  • Does not share any information with third parties

2. Local Data Usage

The App stores data only on your device to enable:

  • Saving your quiz progress
  • Restoring your progress when you reopen the App

No information is sent outside your device.

3. Firebase Usage

The app includes Firebase initialization to support future features. However:

  • No personal information is collected by Firebase
  • No user-specific data is stored or transmitted
  • Firebase is not used for analytics, authentication, or data storage in the current version of the App

4. Children’s Privacy

Sat Math is safe for users of all ages, including children.

The App:

  • Does not collect personal data from children
  • Does not require an account, login, or parental consent
  • Is fully functional without providing any personal information

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party tools, SDKs, analytics services, or advertising networks in its current version.

6. Security

Although the App does not collect or store personal information, we take reasonable steps to protect locally stored progress data on your device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Effective Date”.
